An OS for Your Home

Prodigy is a networked hardware device that enables a hands-free Siri experience for controlling devices and appliances in your home. Our first version of Prodigy is designed to interact directly with speakers and sound systems. Our device will unify control of this functionality under one voice controlled operating system.

Network of Control

Bring all your sound systems onto the cloud, with one Prodigy add-on for each set of speakers. Prodigy lets you set up and control your own private network of devices with just your voice.

Responsive

Ships with an onboard OS that accepts voice commands for music playback and selection, allowing for quick but expressive ways to interact with your smart home and the growing possiblities for interaction with supported third party services (Spotify, etc).

Secure and Private

We understand privacy and security is paramount to our users. Prodigy's microphones turn off on command for the privacy you need. Our technology never records or processes any sound that is not a direct command to Prodigy.
